Philosophy of the restaurant

C'est la vie (in french "that's life") – is a restaurant that will give you a pleasant feeling of the French atmosphere, native comfort, will make you in France at home. True french way of life, inspiring you to love each other and everything around. Imagine: you sit up outside with a view of the city that's wakening up... or in a luxury hall, on the settee with a view of Kiev center. French music sounds and you sense unique strains having fresh baguette (compliment of the chef). In your hand – wineglass of white Cotes du Rhone or red Bourgogne, and in your mind, you are somewhere up outside on one of the streets of the center of Paris.
